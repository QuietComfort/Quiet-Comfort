The Quiet Comfort Project

Handmade comfort for people going through hard moments

What We Do

The Quiet Comfort Project creates and donates handmade quilts and comfort items to people experiencing difficult seasons of life — grief, illness, loss, and other heavy moments.

Each piece is made with care and given freely, with the hope that something handmade can bring a little warmth, softness, and quiet comfort.

Current Project

Our first donation is being prepared for the local animal control shelter, where handmade beds will help give rescued animals a softer place to rest.
