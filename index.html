About The Quiet Comfort Project

Using reclaimed fabric, The Quiet Comfort Project creates quilts, blankets, pet beds, and other handmade items to bring comfort to people and animals facing grief, illness, or hardship.

Each piece is made with care and intention — a reminder that someone sees you, values you, and that you are enough.

Current Project

We are currently making quilts and pet beds for our local Animal Control to help provide warmth and comfort to shelter pets waiting for their homes.

Donate Fabric & Materials

Have extra materials at home? We happily accept sheets, towels, clothing, blankets, cushions, pillows, or fabric of any kind.

These materials are transformed into comfort items for people and animals in need.
